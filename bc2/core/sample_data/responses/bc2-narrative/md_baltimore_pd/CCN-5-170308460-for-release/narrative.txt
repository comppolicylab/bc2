On 3/22/17 at 0412 hours this officer was dispatched to 2400 Guilford for a shooting. Upon responding this officer located a b/m later identified as Alphonza Watson laying on the sidewalk in front of 2400 Guilford on his back with his feet in the street. Mr. Watson was holding his right side and complaining of chest pain and difficulty breathing from an apparent gunshot wound. Medic #16 responded to location and transported Mr. Watson to Johns Hopkins where at 0544 hours Mr. Watson succumbed to his wounds. Dr. Efron pronounced at 0544 hours. Investigation revealed Mr Watson was in the 2400 block of Guilford Ave where he was shot by unknown male who fled the area.
